Taco Trivia

When did the taco first appear in the United States?

The taco is widely considered to have first appeared in the United States around 1905.

  • Context: Mexican migrants brought their traditional food culture to the U.S. in the early 20th century while working on railroads and in mines.
  • Early Popularity: Initially sold by street vendors in cities like Los Angeles, they were an affordable, portable meal for the working class.
